About Elysiandex

Pioneering the future of dexterous robotic manipulation

Our Mission

Elysiandex builds dexterous humanoid robots with human-like, tactile robotic hands. Our platform integrates high-dynamic whole-body control with human-level dexterous manipulation skills for autonomous operation in real-world settings.

We believe that the future of robotics lies in the ability to interact with the world as naturally as humans do. Our technology bridges the gap between robotic precision and human dexterity, enabling machines to perform complex manipulation tasks with unprecedented accuracy and sensitivity.

Our Technology

At the heart of our innovation is the Wave robotic hand - a 22 degrees of freedom, dexterous robotic hand engineered to match the human hand in size, structure, and tactile sensitivity. Combined with our advanced Vision-Tactile-Language-Action (VTLA) architecture, we enable anthropomorphic dexterous manipulation with reflex-level intelligence.

🤖

Human-Like Dexterity

Our Wave hand replicates the human hand's 22 degrees of freedom, enabling natural and precise manipulation of objects in complex environments.

🎯

Tactile Sensitivity

With over 1000 tactile sensors and 0.005N force resolution, our robots can feel and manipulate objects with human-like sensitivity.

⚡

Quasi-Direct Drive

Our innovative 15:1 gear ratio provides the perfect balance of torque and transparency, enabling backdrivable and force-controlled manipulation.

🧠

VTLA Architecture

Vision-Tactile-Language-Action model integrates visual perception, tactile feedback, and language understanding for intelligent manipulation.
