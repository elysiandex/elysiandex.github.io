The Dexterity Deadlock
February 12, 2026 · Quanting Xie, Tongzhou Liao, Yonatan Bisk · Origami Robotics
Robot dexterity is stuck behind one hardware problem: the gearbox. High-ratio gearboxes break sim-to-real transfer, destroy force transparency, and are the first thing to wear out. This post explains why — and what we did about it. Covers the geometric curse (τ ∝ r³), N² impedance mismatch, information-theoretic analysis of actuator transparency, and how axial flux motors enabled a drop from 288:1 to 15:1 gear ratio.
